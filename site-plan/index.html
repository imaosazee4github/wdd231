Global Recipe Hub

Project Site Plan

1. Site Name

Global Recipe Hub

Reason for Selection: This name was chosen because it reflects the international scope of recipes featured on the site while positioning it as a central resource (hub) for cooking enthusiasts. The name is memorable, clearly communicates the site's purpose, and appeals to a diverse audience interested in exploring cuisines from around the world.

Optional domain availability: globalrecipehub.com

2. Site Purpose

Global Recipe Hub serves as a comprehensive cooking resource that provides:

  • Detailed recipes from various international cuisines with step-by-step instructions
  • Nutritional information and dietary categorization (vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, etc.)
  • Cooking tips, techniques, and kitchen equipment recommendations
  • A searchable database of recipes organized by cuisine type, meal category, and cooking time
  • User engagement through a recipe submission form for community contributions

The site aims to make cooking accessible and enjoyable for both beginners and experienced home cooks by providing clear instructions, helpful context, and a well-organized collection of recipes.

3. Scenarios

Scenario 1:

"I want to cook a healthy vegetarian dinner for my family tonight, but I only have 30 minutes. What quick recipes are available that meet these criteria?"

Scenario 2:

"I'm interested in learning how to make authentic Italian pasta from scratch. Does the site provide detailed instructions and tips for traditional pasta-making techniques?"

Scenario 3:

"I have dietary restrictions and need to find recipes that are both dairy-free and nut-free. How can I filter recipes to match my specific dietary needs?"

4. Color Schema

Primary Color: Sage Green

#6B8E7F

Usage: Main headings, navigation links, buttons, and key interactive elements. This calming green evokes freshness and natural ingredients.

Secondary Color: Warm Cream

#F5F1E8

Usage: Page backgrounds, content boxes, and card backgrounds. Provides a warm, inviting backdrop that enhances readability.

Accent Color: Terracotta

#C67B5C

Usage: Call-to-action buttons, hover states, and highlighted content. Adds warmth and draws attention to important elements.

Text Color: Charcoal

#2D3436

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, and general content. Provides excellent readability against light backgrounds.

5. Typography

Playfair Display

Font Family: 'Playfair Display', serif

Usage: Main headings (h1, h2), site logo, and page titles. This elegant serif font adds sophistication and pairs well with food content.

Weights: Regular (400), Bold (700)

Open Sans is a clean, highly readable sans-serif font perfect for body text and longer content.

Font Family: 'Open Sans', sans-serif

Usage: Body text, paragraphs, navigation links, subheadings (h3, h4), and all general content. Ensures excellent readability across all devices.

Weights: Regular (400), Semi-Bold (600)
